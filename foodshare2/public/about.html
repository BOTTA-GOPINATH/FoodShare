About FoodShare

At FoodShare, our mission is to reduce food waste and fight hunger by connecting donors with those in need.

We provide a platform where restaurants, individuals, and organizations can donate surplus food, and food banks and shelters can easily access it.

Our Vision

We aim to create a sustainable system where no food goes to waste and every meal reaches those who need it.
